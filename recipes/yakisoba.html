Yakisoba

Description

Yakisoba is a Japanese stir-fried noodle dish that combines savory, sweet, and tangy flavors. This speedy recipe is perfect for a delicious weeknight meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 packs yakisoba noodles (instant or pre-cooked)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sliced cabbage
  • 1 cup julienned carrots
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup thinly sliced pork or chicken
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • Optional toppings: bonito flakes and pickled ginger

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Stir-fry the meat until cooked through, then add vegetables and cook until they begin to soften.
  3. Add yakisoba noodles to the pan and use chopsticks or tongs to separate and mix them with the meat and vegetables.
  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and oyster sauce. Pour this sauce over the noodles and toss until everything is well coated and heated through.
  5. Serve hot, topped with bonito flakes and pickled ginger if desired.
