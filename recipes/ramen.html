Ramen

Description

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup dish, known for its savory broth and delicious toppings. This easy recipe brings the flavors of ramen to your kitchen in no time!

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of instant ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup shredded cooked chicken
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Optional toppings: seaweed, corn, and chili oil

Steps

  1. Boil the ramen noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a pot, heat the broth over medium heat and stir in soy sauce, miso paste, and sesame oil.
  3. Add mushrooms to the broth and let them simmer until tender.
  4. Divide the cooked noodles into serving bowls and ladle the hot broth over them.
  5. Top each bowl with shredded chicken, boiled egg halves, green onions, and any optional toppings you like.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade ramen!
